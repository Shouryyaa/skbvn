HISTORY OF SKBVN

A strong foundation helps the buildings always stand strong and firm. Established in 1984 by Shree Indore Bartan Nirmata Avam Vikreta Shaikshanik, Avam Parmarthik Trust, the school of Shree Kasera Bazar Vidya Niketan was expected to be the best school in Indore with guaranteed quality education and imperative knowledge modules. The foundation of the school was laid on the positivity of turning it to be the milestone of every children’s life, from their growth to their development into a rewarding personality, Kasera Bazar school has a great treasure of knowledge to give them.

Moreover, we emphasize on the subtle art of changing the tiny tots into future leaders and agents of profitable change. Having our feet embedded in the land of education for 35 Plus years, we have flourished consistent results and achievements in every student’s life by giving them shining opportunities and a spectrum of growth which has helped us enfold ourselves into the best CBSE school in Indore as well as the best institution of ethical education and academic affiliations. Our expert faculties and distinct approach to education have helped us turn the success curve as one of the top CBSE schools in Indore.
